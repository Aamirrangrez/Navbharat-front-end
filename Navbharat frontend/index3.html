Boundary walls may sound unappealing and something that dulls your space’s overall look, but that’s not the case with us. Boundary fencing can range from iron wire fences over the wall to modern-day trends of steel grills and steel boundary wall.

Boundary walls protect your property from intruders, it’s a must for large spaces like godowns and farm-houses. One can get these walls around easily.

The choice depends upon how large the area is and the uses of the area. For example, for a farm-house, an iron boundary fencing works well but for a poultry farm, a metal compound wall suits well. One should know that these walls create a demarcation line. A compound wall enhances aesthetics and ensures privacy but a metal fencing ensures safety and makes trespassing difficult. One can choose a combination for both for their living space.

Whether it is a 10 feet metal compound wall strengthened by a fence within the property that limits access to certain areas such as the swimming pool, the elevation and placement of these boundary walls are decided on the basis of the privacy needs of the owner.
