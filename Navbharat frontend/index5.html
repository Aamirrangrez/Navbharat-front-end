ABOUT FABRICATION WORLD

As the fabrication industry has evolved over the years, we at Fabrication World focus on maintaining international standard requirements to achieve long-lasting partnerships with the most prominent construction ompanies and manufacturers. Our achievements over the years have pushed us to upgrade the quality of our fabrication products for Indian and global clientele.

WHO ARE WE ?

Established in 1984 in Indore, we have managed to work with the most trusted names, looking for Iron and Steel Fabrication expertise. Whether for Automotive, Railways, Engineering Projects, or Agricultural, our dedicated team has been carefully offering quality services that match the diverse requirements in the most efficient manner.

WHAT WE DO?

Fabrication World has a rich history of serving its clients across the world with a comprehensive range of fabrication works, including iron and steel fabrication for medium and large-scale projects. With the strongest quality equipment and state-of-the-art machinery, we ensure bespoke services and strength as every industry demands.

BEHIND THE SCENES/OUR TEAM

Maintaining exceptional quality and customer support, we have a large team of experienced professionals who are qualified and skilled enough to deliver technical support both on-site and off-site. All our project managers hold extensive working experience in the Metal Fabrication and Mechanical Engineering.
